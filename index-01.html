Wyld

Music Festival and Record Label

Initialy called "Chill Masters", Wyld is a music label based in Toulouse, i've worked for them since June 2018. The particularity of this label is that they produce artists from all over the world, with eclectic styles like rock, indie or techno. It's a small structure, with adorable people who loves music and want to share their passion.

Convergences

A festival rised up by students

Convergences is a festival i've worked for in 2015, it was an initiative from students of the Kedge business school, the architechture school of Bordeaux (ENSAP) and some students of my school. My work was directed to the creation of social media contents (gifs, artists announcement etc ..). It was an amazing experience, it was at this time that I realize that I really want to work as a graphist, and create for events like this.

Illustrations

Work as passion

My passion for drawing was early in my childhood, it was more a hobby in the beginning, and a passion years after years. I begin to draw comics strips like Kid Paddle, and this inspired me a lot the style I have today. I love video games, music and skate, so a lot of my illustration are about those themes and inspiration. I also love Japan style, americans comics, in summary all the things that built me in the 90's.
